Cigars

Appearing often on the pages of Cigar Aficionado Magazine, Shaker's is no stranger to the luxury cigar world. In fact, we hold monthly, themed "Cigar Dinners" that date back to 1992, when Ernesto Carillo of La Gloria Cubana fame asked us to host the first one ever. We have upheld the tradition ever since. Our 1905 glass-topped humidor (leftover from our stint as a Prohibition hot spot) houses an assemblage of roughly two dozen handmade, premium cigar brands, running the gamut from light and gentrified smokes to robust and deep flavors. Though many choices are stellar as a standalone, our thoughtfully curated selection is designed to pair best with brown spirits and wines, and aid as a digestif after a fine meal. All of our cigar stand hosts are expertly trained in our extensive collection, and would be delighted to answer any questions you may have, and assist you in finding the perfect pick.
